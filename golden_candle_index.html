// The System

Not a crossover.
An institutional read.

The Golden Candle Indicator doesn't chase momentum. It finds the exact moments when institutional money has swept stop clusters — then scores the reversal probability before showing you a signal.

01

Gold Candles Appear

Doji, shooting star, inverted hammer, spinning top, and tweezer patterns paint gold the moment they close. Large-body momentum candles are excluded — gold means indecision or reversal only.

02

Probability Meter Scores It

11 real-time factors — equal level sweeps, top-down bias, volume, FVGs, time windows, exhaustion, and more — score each gold candle from 10% to 90% for BUY or SELL.

03

Signal Fires When Ready

Only when probability clears your threshold AND you're inside a backtested time window does an arrow appear. Entry, stop, and target print directly on the chart.
